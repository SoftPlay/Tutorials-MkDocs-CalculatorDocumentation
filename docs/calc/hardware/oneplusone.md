# Simple tutorial
## 1+1, the "hello world" of addition
Follow the steps below:

* Push the `1` button with your meat finger.
* Push the `+` button with your meat finger.
* Push the `1` button with your meat finger.
* Push the `=` button with your meat finger.
* Behold the answer, with your meat eyes.
